Welcome to the Miyamoto Laboratory at the University of Electro-Communications (UEC).
We are affiliated with the Department of Informatics (Graduate School of Informatics and Engineering) and the Management Science and Social Informatics Program in Cluster I (School of Informatics and Engineering).

Our research focuses on the mechanisms by which humans perceive a "mind" in artificial objects.

For more details on our research and the projects developed and published by our students, please visit our website or GitHub Organization!
